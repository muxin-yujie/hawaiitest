```

Moonlight reflecting on the water surface, creating a magical silver pathway. 
In the background, the dark Pacific Ocean with Diamond Head mountain silhouette against the starry sky.

Warm candlelight around the pool edge, colorful cocktails on a small table. 
Soft jazz atmosphere, string lights hanging above. 
A few elegant guests in cocktail dresses and suits, blurred in the background.

The scene captures an intimate moment between a mysterious handsome man (30s, mixed race, 
wearing a white linen shirt, sophisticated) and a young Chinese woman (20s, student,

looking at each other with chemistry and romance in the air.

Cinematic lighting, warm golden tones mixed with cool moonlight blue. 
Dreamy atmosphere, bokeh effect from the city lights and candles. 
Photorealistic, highly detailed, 8K resolution, shot on 35mm lens, f/1.8 aperture.

Color palette: Deep navy blue, warm gold, soft pink, silver moonlight, 
tropical night vibes.

Style: Romantic, luxurious, intimate, cinematic, Hawaiian elegance.
--ar 16:9 --v 6.0 --style raw --q 2
```

## 使用建议

### **推荐工具**：
- **Midjourney v6** - 效果最佳，使用完整英文 prompt
- **Stable Diffusion XL** - 使用英文 prompt，可添加 negative prompt
- **DALL-E 3** - 使用英文 prompt，描述更自然
- **文心一格/通义万相** - 使用中文 prompt

### **Negative Prompt（SD 使用）**：
```
ugly, deformed, noisy, blurry, distorted, out of focus, 
bad anatomy, extra limbs, poorly drawn hands, poorly drawn face, 
mutation, mutated, extra fingers, bad proportions, 
disconnected limbs, ugly background, amateur, low quality, worst quality
```

### **参数建议**：
- **宽高比**: 16:9（电影感）或 2.35:1（超宽屏）
- **风格**: Cinematic / Photorealistic / Romantic
- **灯光**: Night / Moonlight / Candlelight / Warm tones
- **镜头**: 35mm 或 50mm, f/1.8（浅景深）
